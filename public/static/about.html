ABOUT LAKSHYA

LBRCE’s National Level Techno–Cultural Fest

Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering, Mylavaram

The Spirit of LAKSHYA

LAKSHYA 2K26 is the flagship National Level Techno–Cultural Fest of LBRCE, curated to celebrate ideas, innovation, and imagination on one vibrant campus. It brings together brilliant minds from diverse branches and institutions to compete, collaborate, and create.

From intense paper & poster presentations and ideathons to high–energy quizzes, coding battles, workshops, and cultural showcases, LAKSHYA is designed as a complete experience – for learners, leaders, and performers alike.

Every edition of LAKSHYA adds a new chapter to the college’s legacy. LAKSHYA 2K26 continues that journey with refreshed themes, industry–aligned problem statements, and a campus-wide carnival that keeps the energy high from the inaugural to the valedictory.

What Drives LAKSHYA

Our Vision

To make LAKSHYA a nationally recognized platform where students translate classroom learning into real–world innovation, cultivate leadership, and build confidence to solve tomorrow’s problems.

Our Mission

To host inclusive, high–impact technical and cultural events that nurture curiosity, collaboration, and competitive spirit while reflecting the ethos and excellence of LBRCE.

FEST PILLARS

LAKSHYA 2K26 is thoughtfully structured into multiple tracks so that every kind of talent – analytical, creative, technical, or artistic – finds the perfect stage to shine.

Technical Arena

Paper & Poster Presentations

Showcase cutting–edge research, innovative prototypes, and problem–solving approaches evaluated by academia and industry experts.

Innovation Hub

Ideathons & Problem Statements

Tackle real–time challenges, pitch bold ideas, and build solutions that could scale from campus projects to impactful products.

Mind Sports

Quizzes & Brain Battles

High–intensity quiz rounds across technology, aptitude, current affairs, and pop culture – perfect for those who love strategy and speed.

Coding Zone

Hackathons & Code Wars

Test your logic, speed, and debugging skills in competitive programming arenas and project–based hackathons.

Cultural Stage

Performances & Pro–Shows

From classical to contemporary, solo to group, LAKSHYA’s cultural nights bring the entire campus together in rhythm and colour.

Carnival Vibes

Stalls, Games & More

Food stalls, fun games, photo spots, and chill zones ensure that participants enjoy every minute they spend at LBRCE during LAKSHYA.
